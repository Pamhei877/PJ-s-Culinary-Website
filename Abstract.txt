This project presents the development of a kitchen recipe website aimed at providing an intuitive and efficient platform for individuals seeking to improve their cooking skills. The website is designed to offer a comprehensive collection of recipes, categorized for easy access, and accompanied by clear, step-by-step instructions. It caters to both novice and experienced cooks by featuring recipes of varying complexity.

Through this project, the focus is placed on creating a user-friendly interface that enhances the culinary experience, making it easier for users to explore, plan, and execute their meals efficiently. The use of Visual Studio Code as the development environment ensures that the codebase remains well-structured and easy to maintain. Overall, the project aims to provide a valuable resource for anyone looking to improve their cooking experience with a reliable and accessible recipe repository.
