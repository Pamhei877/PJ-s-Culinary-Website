Singju

A simple yet delicious Italian dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • 50g Parmesan, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and cook pancetta until crispy.
  4. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the spaghetti.
  5. Add the spaghetti to the pan with pancetta and toss to combine.
  6. Remove the pan from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra cheese and black pepper if desired.

Tips:

To avoid scrambling the eggs, make sure the pan is off the heat before adding the egg mixture. Toss quickly to coat the pasta evenly.
